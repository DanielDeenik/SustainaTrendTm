About Document Analysis

The SustainaTrend™ Document Analysis tool uses advanced AI to analyze your sustainability reports and ESG disclosures. Our system:

  • Extracts key sustainability metrics and KPIs
  • Checks alignment with major reporting frameworks
  • Identifies disclosure gaps and improvement areas
  • Generates executive summaries and visualizations
  • Enables interactive exploration of your data

The analysis results can be used to improve your sustainability reporting, ensure compliance with frameworks, and identify areas for strategic improvement.

Supported Frameworks

ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards)
The EU's comprehensive sustainability reporting framework covering environmental, social, and governance topics.
GRI (Global Reporting Initiative)
The world's most widely used sustainability reporting standards.
TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures)
Framework for climate-related financial risk disclosures.
SASB (Sustainability Accounting Standards Board)
Industry-specific standards for financial material sustainability topics.
CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)
The EU directive that mandates sustainability reporting for certain companies.
SFDR (Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation)
EU regulation for sustainability-related disclosures in the financial services sector.
